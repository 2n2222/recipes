Outback Steakhouse Ranch Salad Dressing

Recipe Type: Salad Dressing

Home >> Recipes >> Outback Steakhouse® >> Outback Steakhouse Ranch Salad Dressing Recipe

Calories: 145

Cook Time: 30 minutes

1 teaspoon Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix (buttermilk recipe)

1 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup buttermilk

1/4 teaspoon coarse grind black pepper

1/8 teaspoon paprika

1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl. Mix well.

2. Cover bowl and chill dressing for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Makes 1 1/2 cups.

Japanese panko Chicken



4 servings Calories 680.


Original Article

Ginger -              2 thumbs

Scallions-           4  pieces

Jasmine rice -   1 cup

Green beans  -   12 oz

Sesame Oil -       2 Tbsp

Sesame Seeds - 2 Tbsp

Chicken Cutlets -20 oz

Sour Cream.      - 4 Tbsp

Panko Crumbs  - 1 cup

Ketchup.             - 2 oz

Dijon Mustard.   - 2 tsp

Soy Sauce.         - 4 tsp

1- Adjust rack to middle postion and preheat oven to 425. Peel Ginger, then mince until you have 2 TBSP.  Thinly slice scallions, keeping the greens and whites separate.

2- Cook Rice- heat a drizzle of oil in medium pot over medium heat. Add minced ginger and scallion whites. Cook, tossing until fragrant, 1-2 minutes. Add rice and toss to coat. stir in 1 cup of water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a gentle simmer. cook until tender, about 15 mins. keep covered until rest of meal is ready.

3- Roast Green Beans- Toss green beans on a baking sheer with a drizzle of oil and a pinch of salt and pepper. roast in oven until tender and crisped, q0-12 minutes. toss in a medium bowl with sesame oil and sesame seeds to liking.

4- Coat Chicken- Pat chicken dry with paper towe. season all over with salt and pepper, then brush with sour cream. Place panko in a shallow dish and sprinkle some salt and pepper. press chicke. into panko, coating all over and pressing to adhere.

5- Cook Chicken - Heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium high heat. [use non stick if possible]  Add chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center and panko is golden brown, 3-4 mins per side. Meanwhile, combine ketchup, mustard, soy sauce , 2 tsp of sugar,  and 2 tsp water in a small bowl.

6- Plate. - Divide rice and green beans between plates. slice chicken and arrange on rice. drizzle sauce over chicken and rice. Sprinkle scallion greens and serve.  extra sauce for dipping!

4 servings Calories 680.

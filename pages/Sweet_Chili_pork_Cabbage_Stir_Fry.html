Sweet Chili pork & Cabbage Stir Fry



4 servings.      830 Calories Each Serv.


Original Article

Jasmine Rice.                - 1 cup

Package of Red Cabbage & carrot mix.

Scallions.                        - 2

Black Soy Sauce.           - 8Tbsp

Sweet Thai Chili Sauce - 2 oz

Ponzu Sauce.                 - 2 Tsp

Rice wine Vinegar.         - 5 Tsp

Ground Pork.                  - 20 oz

Crispy fried onions.       - 2

Cilantro.                           - around an oz.

Butter.                              - 1 Tbsp

1- Cook Rice

wash and dry produce

In a small pot combine rice, water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a low simmer. cook until Rice is tender, 15-18 mins.

Keep covered off heat until ready to serve.

2- While Rice cooks, trim and thinly slice scallions, separating whites from greens.

in a small bowl, combine sweet soy, chili sauce, ponzu and all the vinegar. [ for a sweeter sauce add sugar].

3- Cook pork- heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium high heat. Add scallion white and cook until softened.

About 1 minute.   Add pork and garlic powder; season with salt and pepper. using a spatula, press into an even layer. Cook without stirring, until brown on the bottom. 3-4 minutes. Break up the meeting pieces and continue cooking until pork is cooked through, 1-2 minutes.

Stir in sauce and cook, stirring, until slightly thickened.  1 minute more.

4- Finish and Serve- Stir in red cabbage and carrot mix and scallion greens into pan with pork; stir until just tender, 2mins. Taste and season with salt and pepper. turn off heat.

Fluff Rice with fork stor in 1 Tbsp butter.

Divide Rice between bowls and top stir fry , crispy onions and finish it with a topping of cilantro.

4 servings.      830 Calories Each Serv.

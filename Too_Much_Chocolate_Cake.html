Too Much Chocolate Cake




Original Article

Desserts♥♥


1 (18.25 ounce) Duncan Hines package devil's food cake mix

1 (5.9 ounce) Hersheys package instant chocolate pudding mix

1 cup sour cream

3 / 4  cup vegetable oil

4 eggs

1/2 cup warm coffee (  instant decaf or regular)

1 cups mini semisweet chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Lay eggs out to reach room temperature or place in a bowl with facet hot water

Sift the cake mix

Mix together the cake and pudding mixes, sour cream, oil, beaten eggs and water. Stir in the chocolate chips and pour batter into a well greased 12 cup bundt pan.

Bake for 50 to 55 minutes, or until top is springy to the touch and a wooden toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool cake thoroughly in pan at least an hour and a half before inverting onto a plate If desired, dust the cake with powdered sugar.

**when using 2- 9\" pans cook for 30-34 mins

*** when using 6" pans  fill pans half way cook for 50 -55 mins

chocolate butter cream




Original Article

1 cup butter

1 and 3/4 cup hersheys special dark cocoa powder

1 cup of Heavy Whipping Cream-you may need to add additional tablespoons 1 at a time if seems to thick

2 teaspoons vanilla

5 cups powdered sugar

in a large bowl combine powered sugar and cocoa powder.

beat the butter with electric mixer at medium speed for about one minute.

Reduce the mixing speed to low,and add the sugar/cocoa mixture about 1/2 cup at a time.

halfway through this process,add heavy cream(add additional heavy cream by tablespoons until desired texture)and vanilla.

once all of the ingredients are throughly incorporated.

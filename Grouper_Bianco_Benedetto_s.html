Grouper Bianco Benedetto's

Recipe 1 Yields: 1 serving

Ingredients

18 oz. filet of black grouper (or substitute other fish)

flour

eggs

italian bread crumbs

vegetable oil

1/4 tshallots, finely chopped

1 Tgreen onions, chopped

1 Tbutter

white wine

1/2 C clam juice

1/4 qt heavy cream

1 CRomano cheese

lemon juice

Method

Dust grouper with flour and drop in egg batter, then cover with Italian bread crumbs.

Pan fry in vegetable oil until golden brown on both sides.

Strain oil from pan. Add shallots, chopped green onions and butter.

Deglaze with white wine.

Add clam juice.

Bake in 500 degree oven for 6 to 8 minutes.

Remove fish from oven and plate; reserve mixture.

Add heavy cream, Romano cheese and a dash of lemon juice to mixture.

Reduce to desired thickness.

Pour mixture over grouper and serve. Enjoy!

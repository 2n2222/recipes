iced lemon pound cake



makes 24 slices


Original Article

Desserts♥♥


1 box yellow cake mix

5.1 oz instant lemon pudding mix

1/2 cup vegetable oil

4 large eggs

1/2 cup milk

8 oz sour cream

7 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice with the pulp

ICING

another version

1/2 stick butter

2 cups powdered sugar

4 tbsp lemon juice

1 tsp lemon extract

or

2 1/2 cups powered sugar

3-4 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice

1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Sprays 2 loaf pans generously with cooking spray.

2. place all cake ingredients in no particular order into stand or electric mixer, beating until well combined, about 1 1/2 mins. Divide cake batter between both loaf pans. Let cake stand for one hour. ( the cake should rise) Bake for 45-55 mins until golden. remove and let cool for 15 mins before removing loaves from pan.

3. To prepare icing , place powered sugar into a large bowl. Drizzle in lemon juice stirring until smooth and thick. should be  good drizzle consistency. drizzle icing over cakes and let set for 20 mins for a firm icing.

alternative icing:

Beat butter,powdered sugar,lemon juice and lemon extract until smooth and supple.

Spread evenly on top of both cakes.

Allow glaze to harden before slicing.

makes 24 slices

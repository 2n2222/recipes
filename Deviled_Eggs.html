Deviled Eggs




Original Article

12 hardboiled egg, peeled and chilled

1/2 cup Mayo

1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

1 teaspoon prepared mustard

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

2 green onions, finely chopped

2 tablespoons well-drained pickled sweet relish

paprika to sprinkle the tops

1-place eggs in cold water and bring to a boil

let boil for 3 mins, then take off the stove and cover. It will cook for another 11 mins this way.

2- Transfer eggs into a cold bath. I find that it's easier to peel when eggs have cooled completely.

3- Cut eggs in half and remove yolks. place yolks into a gallon zip lock bag.

4- Place the rest of the ingredients except for paprika into the gallon ziploc. Squeeze the gallon bag to mix ingredients,  continue until throughly incorporated.

5- Cut a small piece of the corner of the ziploc bag and squeeze into the empty white egg halves.

6- Sprinkle with paprika and refrigerate for at least 30 mins.
